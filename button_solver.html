How to use

This follows a strict sequence of guesses to solve the correct sequence in at most 5 total guesses. To use, simply enter the correct value for each guess as you make them in the game.

  1. Guess 1111 in the game and enter the result here.
  2. Guess 1212 and enter the result.
  3. Guess 2112 and enter the result. This will give you the solution, or 4th guess.

Buttons that are greyed out are impossible given the results entered. This shows that you have made a mistake either pressing buttons in game, or entering results here.
